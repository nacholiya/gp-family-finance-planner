Logo Variations

How We Talk

Simple: We say "Beans" instead of "Liquid Assets."

Friendly: Use a warm, informal tone and speak directly to the user. Avoid cold interactions. "We're loading your beans..." instead of "Loading..."

Comforting: We use warm greetings and celebratory milestones.

Secure: Our warmth is backed by the promise of bank-grade local privacy.

Familiar: We use names and treat every activity as an investment.

Brand Voice

Designing the Nook

Use Squircles (high corner radius) for all buttons and cards.

Avoid "Alert Red." Use Heritage Orange for important notifications to keep the mood positive and secure.

Utilize soft "Sky Silk" blue shadows to make the UI feel lightweight.

Goal milestone reached! Your vacation fund is complete.

Punctuate happy moments with celebratory images or animations from the brand assets.
